## Reporting Issues

- **Bugs**: Open an issue in the affected component's repository
- **Security vulnerabilities**: Report privately to security@butlerlabs.dev (see [SECURITY.md](https://github.com/butlerdotdev/butler/blob/main/SECURITY.md))
- **Cross-component issues**: Open an issue in the [umbrella repo](https://github.com/butlerdotdev/butler/issues)
